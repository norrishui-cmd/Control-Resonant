## Quick answer

**Control Resonant releases for Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S on September 24, 2026.** Remedy lists both current Xbox consoles among the launch platforms. No Xbox One version has been announced.

## Is Control Resonant on Xbox Game Pass?

There is **no announced Game Pass release** as of July 17, 2026. A confirmed Xbox launch is not the same as confirmed subscription access. Unless Remedy or Xbox announces otherwise, plan on purchasing the game.

## Series X and Series S performance

Remedy has not published final resolution, frame-rate, or graphics-mode targets for either console. Series S is officially supported, but claims about its visual settings should wait for official specifications or verified launch testing.

## Does it support Xbox One?

No Xbox One edition appears in Remedy's official platform list. The announced Xbox versions are Series X and Series S only. Cloud availability through GeForce NOW does not create an Xbox One-native version.
