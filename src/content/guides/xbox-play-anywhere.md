## Quick answer

**The Xbox Store lists Control Resonant as Xbox Play Anywhere.** That program generally links a supported Xbox console and Windows PC version purchased through the Xbox ecosystem, with saves and entitlements following the same Microsoft account where supported.

This does not mean that a Steam or Epic purchase automatically grants an Xbox copy. Store ecosystems are separate.

## Which platforms are involved?

Control Resonant is officially announced for Xbox Series X|S and Windows PC. The Xbox listing also identifies Xbox Play Anywhere. No Xbox One-native version has been announced.

## Is this cross-play?

No. Control Resonant is a single-player game, and Play Anywhere is a purchase/save ecosystem feature rather than multiplayer cross-play. It should also not be confused with GeForce NOW cloud streaming.

## Does the save transfer to Steam, Epic, or PlayStation?

No cross-store or cross-platform save promise has been published for those versions. Xbox Play Anywhere should only be used to describe the Xbox/Microsoft ecosystem until Remedy explicitly documents broader cross-save.

## What should buyers verify?

Before purchase, confirm that the product page in your region still shows Play Anywhere and check the Windows version's final hardware requirements. Store availability, account rules and offline behavior can change or vary by region.
