## Quick answer

**Jesse Faden remains central to Control Resonant, but Dylan Faden is the sole playable character announced by Remedy.** Jesse is missing when Dylan enters the Manhattan crisis, and finding his sister is one of his driving goals.

## Jesse's position after Control

Jesse is the Director of the Federal Bureau of Control and the protagonist of the first game. Remedy has deliberately kept details of her new journey quiet. Her actions shape Dylan's path even though he is the sibling moving the sequel's story forward.

## Is Jesse playable?

Remedy's co-creative director explicitly describes Dylan as the sole playable character “this time.” No playable Jesse chapter, character swap or co-op role has been confirmed. Her importance to the plot should not be confused with confirmed playability.

## Jesse and Dylan's relationship

The two games form linked but independent sibling stories. The first Control followed Jesse's search for Dylan; Resonant reverses the dynamic as Dylan searches for Jesse. Remedy says both arcs revolve around each other and explore the Fadens' complicated past.

## Do newcomers need Jesse's full history?

No. Resonant is designed as a new entry point, though playing Control adds context for Jesse's role, the Oldest House, the Hiss and Dylan's confinement.
