## Quick answer

**Control Resonant includes collectibles and hidden discoveries across its Manhattan zones.** Remedy says New Game Plus can be used to revisit collectibles missed on the first run. A complete category list, total count and location map have not been published.

## What counts as confirmed

The official world overview promises lore, secrets, hidden discoveries and optional activities in each handcrafted zone. It also mentions Altered Items as things players may chase down. That confirms collectible-style exploration without proving that every lore document, recording or Altered Item uses the same tracking system.

## Do collectibles carry into New Game Plus?

The wording says players can revisit collectibles they may have missed. It does not clearly state whether already collected entries remain marked, whether zone counters carry over, or whether some objects reset. Those details should be tested rather than inferred.

## What a useful collectible guide needs

Each future page should identify the exact zone, nearest checkpoint, route, traversal requirement, interaction prompt and reward or lore entry. Screenshots should show both the environment and map position. Pages with only a collectible name and “location coming soon” will remain noindex.

## Relationship to Artifacts

Untapped Artifacts are found in the world and crafted into equippable Artifacts, but they are part of the build and crafting system. They may overlap with collection behavior without being identical to every collectible category.
