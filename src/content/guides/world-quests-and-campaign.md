## Quick answer

Control Resonant has two primary narrative quest types: **Dylan's Journey**, which is the main campaign, and **World Quests**, which are self-contained narrative stories. Zones also contain combat encounters, environmental challenges, hidden discoveries and other optional activities.

## Dylan's Journey

This is the central story following Dylan Faden through the Manhattan crisis and his search for Jesse. Remedy has not published the full mission list, so chapter names, order and objectives should not be treated as known before launch.

## World Quests

World Quests are distinct narrative stories found outside the main campaign path. “Self-contained” suggests each can deliver its own story, but official material does not yet establish how they are accepted, tracked, failed or replayed.

## Other activities

Remedy has named combat encounters, environmental challenges, hidden discoveries and the pursuit of Altered Items. These activities are woven into handcrafted zones rather than presented as a demand to clear every map marker.
