## Quick answer

**Yes, Control Resonant is coming to Mac, but not on the September 24 launch date.** Remedy says the Mac edition will follow later in 2026 through both Steam and the App Store. No exact Mac date or hardware requirement has been announced.

## Is Mac part of the main launch?

No. PS5, Xbox Series X|S, Windows PC and GeForce NOW are listed for September 24. Mac is a later-2026 release. Treat any more precise date without a Remedy announcement as unconfirmed.

## Which Macs will run it?

The supported chip generations, memory requirements and macOS version are not yet published. It would be unsafe to infer them from another Remedy game. Wait for the official Steam and App Store compatibility fields before purchasing for a specific Mac.

## Will the Steam purchase include Mac?

Steam is confirmed as a Mac storefront, but cross-buy entitlement and save compatibility between Windows and Mac have not been detailed. These questions need explicit store metadata or a Remedy statement.

## What Mac players can do now

If you want to play on September 24 without a console or Windows PC, GeForce NOW is the only confirmed cloud route in the official launch announcement. For the complete release matrix, see the [platform hub](/guides/platforms-and-performance/) and [release-date guide](/release-date/).
