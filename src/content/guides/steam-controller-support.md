## Quick answer

**Steam lists full controller support for Control Resonant, including Xbox and PlayStation controllers.** Remedy recommends playing with a controller. The PS5 version separately confirms DualSense vibration and trigger effects plus advanced remapping.

## What full controller support means

Steam's label indicates the game can be operated with a controller through its supported flow. It does not guarantee that every controller model has platform-specific button icons, haptics or adaptive-trigger behavior on PC.

## Xbox and PlayStation controllers

Both controller families appear in the Steam feature list. Exact support for DualSense adaptive triggers, wired versus wireless advanced features, and third-party pads should be checked against the final PC build and Steam Input settings.

## Why Remedy recommends a controller

Resonant combines standard melee, a chosen Secondary Form, multiple supernatural ability inputs, dodge, double jump, dashes and levitation. A controller provides a consistent layout for simultaneous movement and combat, though this recommendation does not mean keyboard and mouse are unsupported.

## Keyboard and mouse status

Steam does not label a PC game “full controller support” as an exclusion of keyboard and mouse. Final key-remapping depth, mouse acceleration, raw input and icon switching need launch verification.

## Accessibility and remapping

The PlayStation listing confirms fully customizable controls and stick sensitivity/inversion options on PS5. PC settings may overlap but should be documented from its own build.
