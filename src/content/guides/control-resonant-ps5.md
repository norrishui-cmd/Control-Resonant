## Quick answer

**Yes, Control Resonant is coming to PS5 on September 24, 2026.** The US PlayStation Store lists the Standard Edition at $59.99 and the Digital Deluxe Edition at $69.99. Prices vary by region.

The official listing identifies the game as PS5 Pro Enhanced and confirms DualSense vibration and trigger effects. It is a single-player game. There is no announced PS4 version.

## Confirmed PS5 features

- PS5 and PS5 Pro support
- DualSense vibration and trigger effects
- one local player
- digital Standard and Digital Deluxe editions
- physical availability announced by Remedy
- nine listed accessibility features, including volume controls, mono audio, subtitles and large subtitles

Exact quality/performance modes and their frame-rate or resolution targets have not been published. “PS5 Pro Enhanced” confirms enhancements, but it does not by itself establish 4K/60 FPS.

## Is it on PS Plus?

No launch-day PlayStation Plus inclusion has been announced. The game is currently offered as a paid pre-order on the PlayStation Store. Subscription availability can change, so confirm against the store near release.
