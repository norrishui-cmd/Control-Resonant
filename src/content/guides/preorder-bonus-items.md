## Quick answer

The official PlayStation Store lists three digital pre-order rewards for Control Resonant: the **Hiss Corruption Outfit**, **Occult Outfit**, and **Pickpocket’s Tool Artifact**. These are pre-order incentives, separate from the three gameplay items included specifically with the Digital Deluxe Edition.

The two outfits are cosmetic by naming and store classification. The Pickpocket’s Tool is explicitly called an Artifact, placing it in the game’s passive-equipment system. The store currently does not publish its modifier, condition, trade-off, upgrade behavior or whether an equivalent can be earned later. Claims about a currency bonus, loot chance or theft mechanic would therefore be speculation.

## Availability and platform caution

The cited US PlayStation listing confirms the bundle on PS5. Store offers, prices, cut-off times and bonus delivery can vary by platform and region. Before purchasing, check the product page for the exact account region and platform rather than assuming that one storefront’s terms automatically apply elsewhere.

These bonuses do not turn the standard edition into the Digital Deluxe Edition. The latter additionally lists an AWE Mission Outfit, an Untapped Artifact (Wallet), a Starter Resource Bundle, digital artbook and soundtrack; on PS5 it also advertises 48-hour advance access.

## What is still unknown

Remedy has not publicly detailed whether the outfits have color variants, whether the Artifact is exclusive forever, or when the items become usable inside the campaign.
