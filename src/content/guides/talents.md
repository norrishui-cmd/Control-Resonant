## Quick answer

**Talents are one of the three core build pillars in The Gap.** Their role is to strengthen the synergy between Dylan's supernatural Combat Abilities and Aberrant melee attacks. They carry into New Game Plus, where additional talent nodes become available.

## How Talents differ from other systems

- **Combat Abilities** are active supernatural powers, usually earned from Resonants.
- **Weapon Forms** determine how the Aberrant attacks as a Primary, Secondary or Combo Ender.
- **Talents** modify or strengthen the relationships between those active tools.
- **Artifacts** are separate equippable passive items with situational effects and trade-offs.

Keeping these layers separate avoids calling every passive bonus a Talent or assuming an Artifact is part of the talent tree.

## What the talent tree shows

Official interface imagery shows branching nodes connected to weapon-form categories and requirements. One preview image displayed a dodge-related attack node under an Aberrant Strength requirement. Interface names and numerical values seen before launch can change, so this site does not yet publish a complete node database.

## Can all talents be unlocked?

Remedy says the full arsenal cannot be unlocked in a single playthrough. It has not provided a complete first-run talent cap, respec price or node-total table. New Game Plus explicitly adds new nodes, expanding the interactions between abilities and Aberrant attacks.

## How to plan a talent build

Begin with a combat loop rather than an isolated percentage bonus. A heavy-form setup should favor nodes that support its slower impact and survivability; a summon or ability setup should improve the relevant resource and synergy. Exact recommendations must wait for final values and verified testing.
