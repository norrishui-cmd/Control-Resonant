## Quick answer

**Bounties are confirmed optional content in Control Resonant.** Remedy specifically identifies them as activities players may miss on the first playthrough and revisit in New Game Plus. The studio has not yet published the bounty board, target list, reward table or refresh rules.

## Where bounties fit

The game separates its main narrative into Dylan's Journey and World Quests. Bounties sit outside those two primary story categories alongside collectibles and other optional activities. That means a bounty should not automatically be labeled a side quest with a narrative chain.

## Do bounties carry over?

Remedy says New Game Plus gives players an opportunity to revisit bounties they may have missed. It does not explicitly state that completed bounty status transfers, resets, or remains repeatable. Combat progression such as Aberrant upgrades, abilities, talents and artifacts does carry over, while traversal abilities do not.

## What rewards do bounties give?

No official reward table is available. Possible currencies, artifacts, crafting materials or progression points should not be guessed from other games. A reliable bounty page needs the target, acceptance location, objective, difficulty, reward and reset behavior from the release build.
