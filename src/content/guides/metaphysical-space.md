## Quick answer

**Control Resonant includes a mysterious metaphysical space beyond the material world that represents Dylan Faden's troubled psyche.** The Gap is the named dreamlike build space tied to his mind, but official wording does not yet prove that every metaphysical sequence is the same location.

## What is confirmed

Steam's official description says players travel beyond the material world into a space representing Dylan's psyche. Separately, Remedy describes The Gap as a dreamlike place players enter outside combat to shape their build and craft Artifacts.

## Is the metaphysical space a map zone?

Its complete structure is unknown. It may include progression menus, narrative sequences, traversal or other encounters, but those functions should not be merged without evidence. Manhattan zones are physical handcrafted areas; the metaphysical space is explicitly framed as beyond that material world.

## Why Dylan's psyche matters

Dylan spent years confined by the FBC, was possessed by the Hiss and later remained in a coma trapped inside his mind. Making his inner state explorable connects character history to mechanics and environments rather than leaving it only in dialogue.
