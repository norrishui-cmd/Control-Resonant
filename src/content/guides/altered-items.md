## Quick answer

**Altered Items return in Control Resonant as optional discoveries within Manhattan's handcrafted zones.** Remedy mentions chasing down an Altered Item as one example of exploration beyond Dylan's Journey and World Quests. No complete item roster or containment procedure has been revealed.

## What an Altered Item is

In the Control universe, an Altered Item is an ordinary object changed by paranatural forces. These objects can display impossible properties and become subjects of Federal Bureau of Control investigation. This is broader lore context, not proof that every earlier item returns in Resonant.

## Are Altered Items quests or collectibles?

The official overview places them among combat encounters, environmental challenges, hidden discoveries and optional activities. A specific Altered Item might support a quest, puzzle or collectible entry, but the game has not established one universal page type for all of them.

## Will the first game's objects return?

No returning Altered Item list has been announced. The sequel revisits parts of the Oldest House and continues FBC mythology, but individual objects should only receive pages after a confirmed appearance.
