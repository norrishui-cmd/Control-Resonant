## Quick answer

**The PS5 Digital Deluxe Edition of Control Resonant includes 48 hours of advance access.** The US PlayStation Store lists that edition for September 22, 2026 at 2:00 PM UTC, while the Standard Edition is listed for September 24 at the same time.

## Who gets early access?

The official confirmation specifically names the **PlayStation 5 Digital Deluxe Edition**. Buying the PS5 Standard Edition does not include the announced two-day access window.

Steam's current Digital Deluxe description lists the artbook, soundtrack, AWE Mission Outfit, Untapped Artifact and Starter Resource Bundle but does not display the same early-access promise in the retrieved listing. Do not assume the PS5 entitlement transfers to PC or Xbox.

## Is this a demo?

No. Advance access is access to the purchased game before the Standard Edition release time. It is not a free demo, trial or progress-limited preview.

## Regional timing

Store times and calendar dates can vary by region. Remedy notes that the general New Zealand release date is September 25, and local store pages should be treated as the authoritative purchase-time display. The US times above are not a universal local-midnight guarantee.

## What happens after September 24?

The advantage expires once the standard launch begins; Deluxe owners keep the other included digital extras. Compare every item in the [editions and price guide](/guides/editions-price-and-preorder/) and check the main [release-date page](/release-date/) for the platform schedule.
