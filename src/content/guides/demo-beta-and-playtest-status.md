## Quick answer

**No downloadable public demo, beta, open playtest or trial for Control Resonant has been announced as of July 19, 2026.** The official Remedy release announcement, PlayStation store listing and Steam page advertise the full game and pre-orders, but none currently lists a public demo entitlement or beta schedule.

This is a status check, not a claim that a demo can never happen. Pre-release availability can change quickly, so the date above matters. A genuine public test should be traceable to Remedy, an official platform store or a verified Remedy social channel and should specify a platform, access window and download method.

## Why reports mention a “demo”

PlayStation Blog published a Summer Game Fest hands-on report based on a roughly 90-minute **media preview build**. The writer began near the start of the game, tried Dylan’s movement and combat, and finished with a large boss encounter. That controlled press session is evidence about gameplay, but it is not evidence that players can download the same build.

The preview confirmed double dash, double jump, brief levitation, Aberrant primary and secondary attacks, and three mapped paranatural abilities. See the separate [media demo breakdown](/guides/media-demo-hands-on-details/) for those observations.

## How to verify a future announcement

Check the game’s Steam page for a dedicated “Download Demo” control, the PlayStation and Xbox product pages for a trial product, and Remedy’s official game news. Avoid downloads shared through file hosts, unofficial launchers or videos that do not link back to a platform listing. Wishlisting the game is currently the legitimate way to receive storefront updates.

Control Resonant launches September 24, 2026 on PS5, Xbox Series X|S and PC. The announced 48-hour PS5 Digital Deluxe access is paid early access, not a demo.
