## Quick answer

**Zoe De Vera is a Federal Bureau of Control field agent who acts as Dylan Faden's handler in Control Resonant.** Remedy describes their relationship—built around trust, responsibility and learning to rely on other people—as an anchor for the game's emotional core.

## Zoe's role

Zoe has survived the paranatural chaos outside the Oldest House, while Dylan spent years isolated inside it. That contrast gives her practical knowledge of the changed world and gives Dylan a human connection during his deployment into Manhattan.

“Handler” establishes Zoe's professional relationship to Dylan but does not reveal her complete authority, history, or whether she accompanies him physically through every zone. Those details remain part of the story.

## How conversations work

Remedy has introduced a seamless dialogue system that allows conversations to continue during exploration. Zoe's role makes her a likely participant in that communication, but the studio has not published a complete dialogue or companion-system breakdown.

## Is Zoe a playable character or co-op partner?

No such role has been announced. Dylan is the sole confirmed playable character, and the official PS5 listing describes a one-player game. Zoe is presented as an FBC field agent and handler, not as a second human-controlled campaign character.

## What remains unknown

Her earlier FBC assignments, full relationship with Jesse, combat involvement and fate are unrevealed.
