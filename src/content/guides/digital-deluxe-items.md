## Quick answer

The official PS5 product listing names six components: the full **Control Resonant** game, **AWE Mission Outfit**, **Untapped Artifact (Wallet)**, **Starter Resource Bundle**, **Digital Artbook**, and **Original Soundtrack**. The PlayStation package also advertises 48-hour advance access beginning September 22, 2026.

The AWE Mission Outfit is a cosmetic item. “AWE” is the Control universe abbreviation for Altered World Event; the listing does not claim that the outfit adds a mission. The Untapped Artifact (Wallet) belongs to the game’s crafting input class: ordinary Untapped Artifacts are found in the world and crafted into usable passive Artifacts in the Gap. Remedy has not disclosed the resulting Wallet modifier.

The Starter Resource Bundle is described only by name. Its exact resource types, quantities and point of delivery remain unpublished. It should not be marketed as a level skip or exclusive upgrade unless an official description later says so.

## Digital extras versus gameplay items

The artbook and soundtrack are digital media extras, while the outfit, Untapped Artifact and resources connect to the game. None of the official descriptions says that the Deluxe gameplay items are required for story completion, New Game Plus or full access to the core build system.

Pre-order rewards are a separate group: Hiss Corruption Outfit, Occult Outfit and Pickpocket’s Tool Artifact. A player pre-ordering the Deluxe product page may receive both groups where that store explicitly lists them, but offers can vary by platform and territory.

## Before buying

Confirm the price, release time and refund rules on your own regional store. The US PS5 listing showed $69.99 when last reviewed, while the standard edition showed $59.99; those prices are time- and region-sensitive.
